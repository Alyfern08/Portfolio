Design Portforlio Guidelines

What is a design portfolio? A design portfolio a book in which an engineer will formally document, in chronological order, all of his/her work that is associated with a specific design project.

Each engineer has his/her own engineerâ€™s notebook within which he/she records (by hand) project ideas, research, interviews, sketches, calculations, experiments, observations, conclusions, and anything else that is related to a specific design project.

Why? Well, these portfolios are typically required by companies and are recognized as a legal document in patent activities. They also prove the origin of an idea, the diligence in turning an idea into a solution, and prove when an idea became a working solution.
